DRIVING DIRECTIONS

From KL Sentral (Central Hub): Head southwest on Jalan Travers, continue onto Jalan Damansara, merge onto Jalan Sultan Ismail, turn left onto Jalan P. Ramlee, and the hotel will be on your right.

From KLIA/KLIA2 (Airport): Take KLIA Express or a taxi to KL Sentral, then follow the above directions.

From Petronas Twin Towers (KLCC): Go northeast on Jalan Ampang, turn right onto Jalan P. Ramlee, continue straight, and the hotel will be on your left.
